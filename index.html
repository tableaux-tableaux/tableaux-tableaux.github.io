Welcome to Tableaux Tableaux! Here's how to use the collage maker:

  • Click to pick up and drop images.
  • Use the toolbar to refresh images, change the background, or clear the canvas.
  • Click "Preview" to see your collage without the interface.
  • Explore and enjoy crafting your own tableaux!

Tableaux Tableaux

Tableaux Tableaux is an interactive web-based application that proposes collage-making as a tool for citation. Utilizing the pictorial tableau as an aesthetic framework, the application offers an immersive digital creative experience. As you arrange, drag, and layer images, consider the interplay of place-making, memory, and belonging, as it unfolds in your own tableaux.
